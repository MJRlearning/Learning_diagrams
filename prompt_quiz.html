What does "context" mean in AI prompts?

Correct! Context refers to the background information, setting, or circumstances that help the AI better understand what you're asking for and provide more relevant responses.

Question 5 of 8

True or False: Providing examples in your prompt can help improve the AI's output.

True! Examples are incredibly helpful. They show the AI the style, format, and type of response you're looking for. This technique is called "few-shot prompting" and significantly improves output quality.

Question 6 of 8

What should you do if the AI's first response isn't quite what you wanted?

Perfect! Refining your prompt is key to getting better results. Add more details, clarify your requirements, or provide examples. Prompt engineering is often an iterative process.

Question 7 of 8

Which element is LEAST important in a good prompt?

Correct! While politeness is nice, it's the least important for getting good results. AI responds to clarity and specificity rather than social conventions. Focus on clear tasks, context, and requirements.

Question 8 of 8

What's the best approach when asking AI to help with creative tasks?

Excellent! The sweet spot for creative tasks is providing enough constraints to guide the AI (audience, style, length, topic) while still leaving room for creative interpretation. This balance yields the most useful and innovative results.
