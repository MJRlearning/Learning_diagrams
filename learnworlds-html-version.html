AI Agents

Definition: AI Agents are autonomous systems that can perceive their environment, make decisions, and take actions to achieve specific goals.

Key Characteristics:

  • Goal-oriented behavior
  • Learning and adaptation capabilities
  • Natural language interaction
  • Context awareness
  • Independent decision-making
âš¡

AI Automation

Definition: AI Automation uses artificial intelligence to perform specific tasks or processes without human intervention, following predetermined rules and workflows.

Key Characteristics:

  • Rule-based execution
  • Consistent, predictable results
  • Trigger-based activation
  • High efficiency for repetitive tasks
  • Minimal human oversight needed

Curriculum Planning Agent

Analyzes student performance data to suggest curriculum adjustments and personalized learning paths.

Student Support Agent

AI Automation Examples in Education

Grade Book Updates

Automatically transfers quiz scores from learning management system to gradebook when assignments are completed.

Attendance Tracking

Sends automated reminders to students with poor attendance and generates weekly reports for administrators.

Assignment Distribution

Automatically releases assignments based on schedule and sends submission reminder emails.
