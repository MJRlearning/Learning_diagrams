Understanding Generative AI

What does "generative" mean in the context of AI?

In AI context, "generative" refers to artificial intelligence systems that can create new content rather than just analyzing or classifying existing data. These AI models learn patterns from training data and use that knowledge to generate entirely new outputs that didn't exist before - whether that's text, images, music, code, or other content.
